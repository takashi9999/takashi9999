In the United States, where the number of people infected with the new coronavirus has exceeded 20,000 and is now the highest in the world, the number of people infected and dying continues to rise, with more than 700 people dying for five consecutive days in Eastern New York State.

新型コロナウイルスの感染者数が２万人を突破し、世界で最も多い感染者数となった米国では、ニューヨーク州東部で５日連続で７００人以上が死亡するなど、感染者数や死亡者数の増加が続いています。

「In the United States, where the number of people infected with the new coronavirus has exceeded 20,000 and is now the highest in the world, the number of people infected and dying continues to rise, with more than 700 people dying for five consecutive days in Eastern New York State.」「ー新型コロナウイルスの感染者数が２万人を突破し、世界で最も多い感染者数となった米国では、ニューヨーク州東部で５日連続で７００人以上が死亡するなど、感染者数や死亡者数の増加が続いています。」

According to the Johns Hopkins University in the U.S., which summarizes the world's infection situation, as of 5:00 a.m. on the 12th, Japan time, the number of people infected with the new coronavirus was 514,415 and the number of people who died was 20,71, both of which were the highest in the world.
